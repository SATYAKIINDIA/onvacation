5 must-see spots in Udaipur

By OnVacation · Sep 1, 2025 · 4 min read

Udaipur — a city of lakes and palaces. We visited in the golden hour and found these five unforgettable spots.

1. City Palace

Start early to catch the morning light and avoid crowds.

2. Lake Pichola boat ride

Take a short boat trip at sunset; it's magical for photos.

3. Jagdish Temple lookout

A compact viewpoint — good for city panoramas.

4. Vintage cafes

Sample local snacks and tea at a rooftop cafe.

5. Markets at night

Markets are lively in the evening — great for souvenirs and street food.
